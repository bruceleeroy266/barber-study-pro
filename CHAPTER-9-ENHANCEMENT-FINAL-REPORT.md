### Primary Lesions (9 cards) - STATE BOARD CRITICAL

**All 9 Primary Lesions Covered:**
1. **Macule** - flat discolored spot (freckles)
2. **Papule** - small raised bump (warts, moles)
3. **Pustule** - pus-filled lesion (acne, impetigo)
4. **Vesicle** - small fluid blister (herpes, chickenpox)
5. **Bulla** - large fluid blister (friction blisters)
6. **Wheal** - temporary swelling (hives, allergic reactions)
7. **Nodule** - large deep bump (cysts, deep acne)
8. **Cyst** - closed sac with fluid/solid (sebaceous cyst)
9. **Tumor** - abnormal mass (benign or malignant)

**Quality Features:**
