## DISCORD COMMUNITY: "OKC Barber Study Group"

### Purpose
- Free community for OKC barber students
- Study together, share resources
- Support network
- Builds trust before selling

### Channels Structure
**WELCOME**
- #rules — Basic guidelines
- #introduce-yourself — Name, school, hours completed

**STUDY**
- #written-exam-prep — Questions, discussions
- #practical-exam-prep — Video shares, technique tips
- #oklahoma-laws — State-specific rules
- #study-buddies — Find partners

**RESOURCES**
- #shared-notes — Community contributions
- #video-links — Helpful YouTube videos
- #questions — Ask anything

**GENERAL**
- #general-chat — Off-topic
- #wins — Pass the exam? Celebrate here
- #jobs — Hiring/leads after licensing

### Roles
- @Student — Default role
- @Graduate — Passed the exam
- @Licensed Barber — Working professionals
- @Moderator — You + trusted members

### Launch Strategy
1. Start free, invite classmates
2. Build value, establish yourself as resource
3. Launch paid study guide to Discord members first
4. Offer Discord as bonus with purchase
