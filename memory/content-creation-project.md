# Gabe's Content Creation Project

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** Gabe (Gabriel Ark)
- **Background:** Navy veteran (17 years), 30 years martial arts (17 jiu jitsu), 3 daughters
- **Current:** Barber school (800 hours remaining), 3 years until military retirement
- **Angle:** Disciplined Dad — veteran discipline + dad life + jiu jitsu/fitness over 35

## Video 1: "Who I Am" (Intro Video)

**Title:** "36, Navy, 3 Kids, Starting Over — Here's Why I'm Doing This"

**Runtime:** ~3 minutes

**Script:**

**[0:00-0:20] HOOK**
*(Just talking, no fancy intro)*

"Alright, so... 36 years old. Three daughters. 17 years in the Navy. Another 17 doing jiu jitsu. And I'm about to walk away from a paycheck to cut hair for a living."

"Sounds crazy when I say it out loud."

**[0:20-0:55] WHO YOU ARE**
"I'm Gabe. Joined the Navy at 19 because... honestly, I didn't know what else to do. Figured I'd do my four years, get out, be done with it."

"That was 17 years ago."

"Somewhere in there I found jiu jitsu. Kept me sane, kept me in shape, kept me from doing something stupid on weekends."

**[0:55-1:30] THE REALITY**
"Now I've got three kids. Ages 10, 9, and 1. And I'm tired of missing stuff. Tired of deployments, tired of early morning musters, tired of someone else owning my time."

"So I'm doing this thing where I become a barber. 800 hours of school left. Building a business for when I retire in three years."

**[1:30-2:10] WHY THIS CHANNEL**
"But here's the thing — I'm not seeing people like me on YouTube. You got your 22-year-old fitness influencers. You got your professional athletes. You got your 'I quit my job to travel the world' people."

"Where's the content for the guy who just wants to stay in shape, keep training, build something, and actually see his kids grow up?"

"So I'm making it. Documenting what works, what doesn't, all of it."

**[2:10-2:45] WHAT TO EXPECT**
"We're talking jiu jitsu for busy people. Fitness when you're not young anymore. Starting a business while working full-time. Being a dad without losing yourself."

"No BS. No 'hustle harder' motivational crap. Just real talk from someone in the trenches."

**[2:45-3:00] CLOSE**
"If that sounds like your life — or where you're headed — hit subscribe. Drop a comment, let me know what you're dealing with. We'll figure it out together."

"See you in the next one."

## 90-Day Content Plan

### Month 1: Foundation
- Set up YouTube + Instagram
- 4 videos: Intro/who you are, "Why I started jiu jitsu at [age]", "Military discipline for civilians", "Training with 3 kids"
- Post 2x/week on Instagram (training clips, mindset quotes)

### Month 2: Value Content
- 4 videos: "Jiu jitsu for busy dads", "Fitness at 36", "Morning routine", "Rolling after 17 years"
- Start "Technique Tuesday" shorts

### Month 3: Community Building
- 4 videos: "What 30 years of martial arts taught me", "Transitioning careers", "Barber school + military", Q&A
- Engage in comments, build email list

## Equipment Needed
- Phone + tripod
- Good lighting (window light works)
- Quiet space

## Notes
- Tone: Real, conversational, no BS
- Target: Men 30-50, veterans, dads, career transitioners
- Differentiators: Navy vet + dad + jiu jitsu + barber school + fitness journey
