Implements, Tools & Equipment

Straight Razor

A sharp blade that folds into its handle. Used for shaving facial hair. Requires skill and proper sanitation.

2/18

Shears

Click to reveal

Cutting instruments with two blades joined by a screw. Used for cutting hair. Various sizes for different techniques.

3/18

Thinning Shears

Click to reveal

Shears with teeth on one blade used to remove bulk and blend hair without changing the overall length.

4/18

Clippers

Click to reveal

Electric or manual cutting tools with oscillating blades. Used for cutting large amounts of hair quickly.

5/18

Trimmers

Click to reveal

Smaller electric cutting tools for detailed work. Used for outlining, edging, and detailing haircuts.

6/18

Clipper Guards

Click to reveal

Attachments that fit over clipper blades to control cutting length. Numbered by length (e.g., #1, #2, #3).

7/18

Comb

Click to reveal

Tool with teeth used for detangling, sectioning, and guiding hair during cutting. Various types for different purposes.

8/18

Barber Chair

Click to reveal

Specialized chair that adjusts for height and reclines. Should be sanitized between each client.

9/18

Neck Strips

Click to reveal

Paper or tissue strips placed around the neck before the cape. Provides hygiene barrier and absorbs moisture.

10/18

Barber Cape

Click to reveal

Protective cloth worn by clients during services. Should be changed or sanitized between clients.

11/18

Lather Brush

Click to reveal

Brush used to apply shaving cream or lather. Typically made with boar or synthetic bristles.

12/18

Strop

Click to reveal

Leather strap used to sharpen and smooth razor blades before shaving. Maintains blade edge.

13/18

Hot Towel Cabinet

Click to reveal

Heated cabinet that keeps towels warm and moist. Used for facial treatments and preparing skin for shaving.

14/18

Sanitizing Jar

Click to reveal

Container filled with disinfectant solution for storing and sanitizing combs and implements between uses.

15/18

Neck Duster

Click to reveal

Soft-bristled brush used to remove loose hair from client's neck and face after cutting.

16/18

Hair Dryer

Click to reveal

Electric device that blows heated air to dry hair. Nozzle directs airflow for styling control.

17/18

Sectioning Clips

Click to reveal

Clips used to hold sections of hair out of the way while working on other areas. Keeps work organized.

18/18

Blade Disposal Container

Click to reveal

Puncture-resistant container for safe disposal of used razor blades. Required for OSHA compliance.
