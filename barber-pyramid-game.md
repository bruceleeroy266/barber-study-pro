# $100,000 Barber Pyramid - Game Design

## Game Structure

### Teams
- 2 players per team (or 3 with rotating partners)
- One gives clues, one guesses
- Switch roles each round

### The Pyramid Board
6 categories arranged in a pyramid (2-3-1 or 2-2-2 structure):
- Bottom row: Easier, broader terms
- Top: Harder, more specific
- Winner's circle: Single high-value category

## Game Rules

### Giving Clues
- CAN describe, act out, use synonyms
- CANNOT say the word itself
- CANNOT use "rhymes with" or "sounds like"
- CANNOT use parts of the word ("hair" for "haircut")

### Scoring
- Each word guessed correctly: 10 points
- Complete a category: Bonus 20 points
- Winner's circle (all 6 words in 60 seconds): $100,000 (or bragging rights)

### Time Limits
- Regular categories: 30 seconds
- Winner's circle: 60 seconds

---

## Study Mode Variations

### Speed Round
- 60 seconds, as many words as possible across random categories
- Good for quick review

### Category Focus
- Drill one category until mastered
- Good for test prep

### Picture Pyramid
- Show images instead of words
- Clue-giver describes what they see
- Guesser names the term

### Reverse Pyramid
- Guesser sees the word, gives clues
- Clue-giver has to guess
- Tests both sides of knowledge
