Chapter 13: Men's Facial Massage and Treatments

Click any card to flip and reveal the definition

Facial Massage

Manipulation of facial muscles and skin to improve circulation, tone muscles, and promote relaxation.

Facial Treatment

A service designed to cleanse, exfoliate, and improve the condition of facial skin.

Hot Towel Treatment

The application of warm, moist towels to soften beard hair and open pores before shaving.

Facial Steam

The use of warm steam to open pores, soften skin, and prepare the face for treatment.

Exfoliation

The removal of dead skin cells from the surface of the skin to reveal fresher skin beneath.

Facial Mask

A product applied to the face to treat specific skin conditions such as dryness or oiliness.

Moisturizer

A product that hydrates the skin and helps maintain its natural moisture barrier.

Toner

A liquid product used after cleansing to restore the skin's pH balance and tighten pores.

Astringent

A solution that tightens skin and reduces oil production; often contains alcohol.

Massage Cream

A lubricating product used during facial massage to reduce friction and nourish skin.

Beard Oil

A conditioning product that moisturizes facial hair and the skin beneath the beard.

Beard Balm

A styling and conditioning product that provides light hold while moisturizing beard hair.

Skin Analysis

Examination of the skin to determine type, condition, and appropriate treatments.

Oily Skin

Skin type characterized by overactive sebaceous glands producing excess oil.

Dry Skin

Skin type lacking sufficient moisture, often feeling tight and showing fine lines.

Combination Skin

Skin type with both oily and dry areas, typically oily in T-zone and dry on cheeks.

Sensitive Skin

Skin type that reacts easily to products or environmental factors with redness or irritation.

Normal Skin

Balanced skin type with good oil-moisture balance, small pores, and even texture.

Comedone

A clogged pore; blackheads (open) and whiteheads (closed) are types of comedones.

Acne

A skin condition characterized by inflamed pores, pimples, and sometimes cysts.
