Chapter 21: The Business of Barbering

Click any card to flip and reveal the definition

Sole Proprietorship

A business owned and operated by one person who is personally liable for all debts.

Partnership

A business owned by two or more people who share profits, losses, and responsibilities.

Corporation

A legal entity separate from its owners; provides limited liability protection.

LLC

Limited Liability Company; combines partnership flexibility with corporation liability protection.

Booth Rental

An arrangement where barbers rent space in a shop and operate as independent contractors.

Commission

A percentage of service revenue paid to barbers as compensation; typically 40-60%.

Salary

A fixed regular payment made to employees regardless of services performed.

Independent Contractor

A self-employed barber who controls how services are performed and sets their own schedule.

Business Plan

A written document outlining business goals, strategies, and financial projections.

Target Market

The specific group of customers a business aims to serve with its services.

Marketing

Activities that promote services and attract clients to the business.

Social Media Marketing

Using platforms like Instagram and Facebook to showcase work and attract clients.

Retail Sales

Selling professional products to clients; adds revenue and improves client retention.

Operating Expenses

Regular costs of running a business including rent, utilities, supplies, and insurance.

Profit and Loss Statement

A financial report showing revenue, expenses, and net profit or loss over a period.

Tax Deductions

Business expenses that can be subtracted from income to reduce tax liability.

Continuing Education

Ongoing training required to maintain licensure and stay current with industry trends.

Bookkeeping

The recording of financial transactions including income, expenses, and payroll.

Liability Insurance

Coverage that protects against claims of injury or damage caused by business operations.

Professional Portfolio

A collection of photos showcasing a barber's best work for marketing and career advancement.
