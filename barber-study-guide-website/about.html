About the Guide

The story behind the most comprehensive barber study guide available

Why This Guide Exists

The Barber Study Guide was born out of frustration. As a barber student, I found myself overwhelmed by the sheer amount of information I needed to memorize for the state board exam. The textbooks were dense, the notes were scattered, and I couldn't find a single resource that organized everything in a way that made sense.

So I decided to create it myself.

This guide is the resource I wish I had when I started barber school. It's comprehensive yet digestible, thorough yet organized. Every chapter is designed to help you not just memorize facts, but actually understand the concepts that will make you a better barber.

About the Author

Gabe — Barber Student & Creator

I'm Gabe, a barber student with 17 years of military service under my belt. After nearly two decades of serving my country, I'm transitioning into a new career that I'm passionate about: barbering.

Currently completing my 1,500 hours of barber school (700 down, 800 to go), I've experienced firsthand the challenges of balancing school, service, and family life. With three daughters at home, I understand what it's like to study efficiently—because free time is precious.

This guide combines my military discipline with my barber school experience. I believe that good information, well-organized, can make the difference between passing and failing, between confidence and anxiety.

My Mission

"To help every barber student pass their state board exam with confidence, regardless of their background or learning style."

I believe that becoming a barber should be accessible to everyone. The state board exam is a hurdle, but it shouldn't be a wall. With the right preparation, anyone can pass—and this guide is designed to be that preparation.

Whether you're fresh out of high school, transitioning careers like me, or returning to the workforce after time away, this guide meets you where you are and helps you get where you want to be.
