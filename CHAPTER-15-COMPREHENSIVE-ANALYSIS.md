**1. HAIR REPLACEMENT INDUSTRY & CONSULTATION**
- Fastest-growing market category (second only to haircutting)

**2. ALTERNATIVE METHODS**
- **Topical Medications:**
  - Minoxidil (2% solution, FDA approved for hair growth)
  - Finasteride (oral medication, prescription)
- **Low-Light Laser Therapy:** FDA approved, stimulates circulation
- **Surgical Restoration:**
  - Hair transplantation (follicle removal/transplanting)
  - Scalp reduction (removing bald area, pulling hair together)
  - Flap surgery (attaching hair-bearing skin flap)
- **Nonsurgical Options:**
  - Fiber cover-up products
  - Root-turning technique

**3. HAIR REPLACEMENT MATERIALS**
- **Human Hair:** Natural look, can be chemically processed, imported/graded, requires maintenance
- **Synthetic Hair:** High gloss or matte, tangle-resistant, loses style easily, cleaner solutions
- **Mixed Hair:** Blend of human/synthetic or animal hair
- **Base Construction:** Hard, soft, mesh, net, polyurethane, combination
- **Knotting Methods:** Single knotting, V-looping, single hair injection

**4. STOCK VS CUSTOM SYSTEMS**
- **Pre-custom (Stock):** Sample systems clients can try before ordering
- **Custom Systems:** Tailored to individual specifications
- Color-matching and customization processes
- Template creation for precise fitting

**5. MEASURING & TEMPLATE CREATION (Procedure 15-1)**

- Template documentation

**6. AFFIXING SYSTEMS**
- **Full Head Bonding (Procedure 15-3):** Complete coverage

- **Facial Hair Solutions:** Spirit gum attachment for mustaches/beards

**9. BASIC CARE & MAINTENANCE**
- Use manufacturer-recommended products only
- Block placement when not worn
- Remove for swimming/showering
- Clean every 3-4 weeks
- Never fold systems
- Light styling products, plain water setting
- Prevent dryness/brittleness with reconditioner

**10. RECONDITIONING SYSTEMS**
- Small amounts of reconditioner to prevent dryness
- Color rinse for yellowing hair
- Procedure 15-8: Coloring Hair Replacement Systems

**11. PERMANENT WAVING SYSTEMS (Procedure 15-9)**
- Natural look blending
- Rod placement technique
- T-pin securing method
- Roller picks for support
- Requires time, creativity, attention to detail

**12. CUTTING, TAPERING, BLENDING**
- **Remove method:** Fingers-and-shear at 90-degree angle
- **Comb method:** Blend sides from temple to sideburn
- Taper to natural hairline
- Create natural appearance with gradual transitions

**13. CLEANING PROCEDURES**
- **Procedure 15-6:** Cleaning Human Hair Systems
- **Procedure 15-7:** Cleaning Synthetic Wigs
- Use appropriate solvents for material type
- T-pin securing during cleaning
- Lukewarm water rinsing
- Two wigs recommended (one serviced, one worn)
