Chapter 12: Treatment of the Hair and Scalp

Click any card to flip and reveal the definition

Scalp Analysis

Examination of the scalp to determine its condition, type, and any disorders before treatment.

Hair Analysis

Evaluation of hair texture, porosity, elasticity, and condition to determine appropriate treatments.

Shampoo

A product used to cleanse the hair and scalp by removing dirt, oil, and product buildup.

Conditioner

A product that restores moisture, adds shine, and improves the manageability of hair.

Scalp Treatment

A service designed to improve the condition of the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Mask

A deep conditioning treatment that penetrates the hair shaft to repair and nourish damaged hair.

Hot Oil Treatment

A conditioning treatment using heated oil to penetrate the hair cuticle and restore moisture.

Scalp Massage

Manipulation of the scalp to increase blood circulation and promote relaxation.

Effleurage

Light, continuous stroking massage movement used to relax and increase blood flow.

Petrissage

A kneading massage movement that stimulates deeper tissues and muscles.

Tapotement

A stimulating massage movement using light tapping or slapping motions.

Friction

A deep rubbing massage movement used to generate heat and stimulate the scalp.

Vibration

A rapid shaking or trembling massage movement used to stimulate nerves and muscles.

pH Balance

The acidity or alkalinity of hair products; balanced pH helps maintain healthy hair condition.

Humectant

An ingredient that attracts moisture from the air to hydrate the hair and scalp.

Emollient

An ingredient that softens and smooths the hair by filling in gaps in the cuticle.

Protein Treatment

A treatment that strengthens hair by depositing protein to rebuild damaged areas.

Moisturizer

A product that adds and retains moisture in the hair to prevent dryness and breakage.

Clarifying Shampoo

A deep-cleansing shampoo that removes heavy product buildup and impurities.

Medicated Shampoo

A shampoo containing active ingredients to treat specific scalp conditions like dandruff.
