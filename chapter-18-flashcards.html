Chapter 18: Men's Chemical Texture Services

Click any card to flip and reveal the definition

Chemical Texture Service

Services that alter the natural wave pattern of hair through chemical processes.

Permanent Waving

A chemical service that creates curls or waves in straight hair using rods and waving lotion.

Chemical Relaxing

A process that permanently straightens curly or coily hair using chemical solutions.

Waving Lotion

A chemical solution that breaks down the hair's disulfide bonds to allow reshaping.

Neutralizer

A solution that reforms the disulfide bonds in their new position to lock in the curl or straightness.

Disulfide Bonds

Strong chemical side bonds that join the sulfur atoms of two cysteine amino acids to create cystine.

Perm Rod

Plastic or foam tools used to wrap hair and determine the size and shape of curls.

End Papers

Thin papers used to wrap around hair ends to protect them during the perm process.

Base Section

The subsection of hair on which the perm rod is placed during wrapping.

Alkaline Perm

A permanent wave with a pH between 9.0-9.6; processes quickly and produces firm curls.

Acid Perm

A permanent wave with a pH between 6.5-7.5; gentler on hair and produces softer curls.

Exothermic Perm

A self-heating permanent wave that produces heat through a chemical reaction.

Thio Relaxer

A chemical relaxer using ammonium thioglycolate; milder than lye relaxers.

Hydroxide Relaxer

A strong chemical relaxer using sodium, potassium, or lithium hydroxide; also called lye relaxer.

Base Cream

A protective cream applied to the scalp before chemical relaxer application.

Processing Time

The amount of time a chemical solution remains on the hair to achieve desired results.

Test Curl

Unwrapping one rod during processing to check if the curl pattern is forming properly.

Underprocessed

Hair that has not been exposed to the chemical solution long enough to achieve desired results.

Overprocessed

Hair that has been exposed to chemicals too long, resulting in damage and weak curls.

Texture Softener

A mild chemical service that loosens curl pattern without fully straightening hair.
