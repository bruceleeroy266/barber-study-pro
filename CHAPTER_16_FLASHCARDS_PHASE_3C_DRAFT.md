## 50. Adapting to Texture and Density

**Front:**  
How should a barber adjust sectioning and technique based on hair **texture** and **density**?

**Back:**  
Fine or thin hair needs smaller sections and conservative layering to preserve fullness. Coarse or thick hair needs controlled sectioning and may require weight release.

## 51. Curly Hair Consultation

**Front:**  
How should a barber respond when a curly-haired client brings a straight-hair inspiration photo?

**Back:**  
The barber should analyze the curl pattern and texture, explain how shrinkage will affect the result, and adapt the design rather than copying the photo exactly.
