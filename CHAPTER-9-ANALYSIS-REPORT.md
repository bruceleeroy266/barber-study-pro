### Why Study the Skin for Barbers (from source):
**Critical Professional Reasons:**
- Skin composition and structure crucial for facials, scalp treatments, haircuts
- Must recognize and differentiate normal vs. abnormal skin conditions
- Must identify conditions requiring medical treatment
- Must recognize conditions that prohibit certain services

**Key Concepts from Introduction:**
- Dermatology: medical science pertaining to skin's nature, structure, functions, diseases, treatment
- Dermatologist: physician specializing in skin treatment
- Skin is body's first line of defense against disease
- Protects organs and creates environmental barrier
- Healthy skin: moist, soft, flexible, smooth, fine-grained
- Slightly acidic pH provides protective barrier
- Should be free of blemishes and disorders
- Skin thickness varies across body
- Callus formation from continued pressure
- Skin: epidermis and dermis divisions
- Appendages: hair, nails, sweat glands, oil glands
