Chapter 20: Working Behind the Chair

Click any card to flip and reveal the definition

Client Consultation

A conversation with the client to understand their needs, expectations, and hair history.

Client Record Card

A document that tracks client information, services performed, and product formulas used.

Active Listening

Fully concentrating on what the client is saying rather than just passively hearing.

Open-Ended Questions

Questions that require more than a yes/no answer and encourage detailed responses.

Visual Aids

Photos, magazines, or digital images used to help clients communicate desired styles.

Hair Analysis

Assessment of hair texture, density, porosity, and elasticity before performing services.

Scalp Analysis

Examination of the scalp for conditions that may affect service choices or outcomes.

Service Recommendation

Professional suggestions for treatments or services based on client needs and hair condition.

Product Recommendation

Suggesting appropriate retail products to help clients maintain their style at home.

Release Form

A document clients sign acknowledging they understand risks of chemical services.

Patch Test

A skin sensitivity test performed 24-48 hours before chemical services to check for allergies.

Strand Test

A test performed on a small section of hair to predict processing time and results.

Elasticity Test

Stretching a wet hair strand to determine its strength and ability to withstand chemical services.

Porosity Test

Sliding fingers up a hair strand to feel the cuticle and determine how hair absorbs moisture.

Pre-Service Ritual

Consistent preparation steps performed before each service to ensure quality and sanitation.

Post-Service Ritual

Steps taken after service completion including styling, education, and retail recommendations.

Rebooking

Scheduling the client's next appointment before they leave the shop.

Referral

A new client who comes to the shop based on a current client's recommendation.

Retail Sales

Selling professional hair care products to clients for home use.

Client Retention

The ability to keep clients returning for services; key to building a successful business.
