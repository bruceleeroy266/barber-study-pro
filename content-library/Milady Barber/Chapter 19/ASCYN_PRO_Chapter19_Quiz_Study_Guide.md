## Question 1 — Q19-01

**Question:** Before you can be hired as a barber, which milestone must you typically complete first?

**Correct Answer:** **A. Pass your state licensing examinations and obtain your barber license**

**Explanation:** Licensure is the gateway to employment. Most states require candidates to pass written and practical examinations, apply for a license, and pay the required fee before legally working as a barber.

## Question 5 — Q19-05

**Question:** You encounter a multiple-choice item where two answer choices say exactly the same thing. Using deductive reasoning, what can you logically conclude?

**Correct Answer:** **B. Both identical choices can be eliminated because they cannot both be the unique answer**

**Explanation:** When two options are identical, they cannot each be the single best answer. Eliminating them narrows the field and improves your chance of selecting the correct remaining option.

## Question 15 — Q19-15

**Question:** An interviewer asks, "How old are you?" This question is generally inappropriate because:

**Correct Answer:** **B. It may lead to illegal age discrimination in hiring decisions**

**Explanation:** Except where the law requires verification that a worker is old enough to be employed, questions about age are improper and can support discriminatory hiring. Respond politely and professionally without being confrontational.
