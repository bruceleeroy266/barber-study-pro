Face Shape Guide

Find the perfect haircut for your features

Not sure what face shape you have? Look straight ahead in a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a dry-erase marker (on the mirror). Compare the shape to the guides below.

⬭

Oval Face

Characteristics: Balanced proportions, slightly wider cheekbones, gently rounded jaw and forehead.

The Good News: This is the most versatile face shape — almost any haircut works!

✅ Best Styles:

  • Classic side part
  • Undercut
  • Pompadour
  • Crew cut
  • Textured crop
  • Any fade type

⚠️ Avoid:

Heavy fringes that cover too much of your forehead — they can make your face look rounder.

⭕

Round Face

Characteristics: Full cheeks, soft angles, face is as wide as it is long.

Goal: Add height and angles to create the illusion of length.

✅ Best Styles:

  • High fades (add height)
  • Pompadour or quiff
  • Textured top with volume
  • Side-swept styles
  • Faux hawk
  • Hard parts (add angles)

⚠️ Avoid:

Full, rounded beards; buzz cuts; and styles with too much width on the sides.

⬜

Square Face

Characteristics: Strong, angular jawline, broad forehead, face is nearly as wide as it is long.

Goal: Soften the angles while highlighting that masculine jaw.

✅ Best Styles:

  • Side part (classic)
  • Crew cut
  • Buzz cut
  • Textured crops
  • Medium length with layers
  • Beard (fills jawline nicely)

⚠️ Avoid:

Very defined, sharp styles that emphasize angles — go for softer, textured looks.

▭

Oblong Face

Characteristics: Face is longer than it is wide, strong jawline, forehead and jaw similar width.

Goal: Add width to the sides and avoid adding height.

✅ Best Styles:

  • Fringes or bangs (shorten face)
  • Side-swept styles
  • Medium length on sides
  • Textured crops
  • Low fades (not high)
  • Side parts with volume on sides

⚠️ Avoid:

High pompadours, spiky styles, and anything that adds height on top.

♥

Heart Face

Characteristics: Wider forehead and cheekbones, narrow, pointed chin.

Goal: Add width at the jawline to balance the wider forehead.

✅ Best Styles:

  • Medium length on top
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Textured fringe
  • Beard or stubble (adds jaw width)
  • Low fades
  • Styles with volume at the nape

⚠️ Avoid:

Very short, tight sides that emphasize the narrow chin; high fades.

◆

Diamond Face

Characteristics: Narrow forehead and jawline, wide cheekbones (highest point of face).

Goal: Minimize wide cheekbones, add width at forehead and chin.

✅ Best Styles:

  • Fringes (cover narrow forehead)
  • Textured crops
  • Side-swept styles
  • Medium length on top
  • Beard (adds jaw width)
  • Styles with volume on top

⚠️ Avoid:

Very short sides that emphasize wide cheekbones; sleek, tight styles.

💡 Quick Tips for Everyone

Bring a Photo

A picture is worth a thousand words. Show Malenny exactly what you're thinking.

Be Realistic

Your hair type and lifestyle matter. A high-maintenance style won't work if you don't style it.

Trust Your Barber

Malenny can suggest modifications to make any style work for your specific features.

Consider Maintenance

Fades need touch-ups every 2-3 weeks. Longer styles can go 4-6 weeks.
