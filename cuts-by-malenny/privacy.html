Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information

Your Privacy Matters

At Cuts by Malenny, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and booking services.

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name: First and last name for appointment identification
  • Phone Number: For appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Email Address: For booking confirmations and marketing (if opted in)
  • Appointment History: Services booked, dates, and preferences

Payment Information

  • We use Stripe to process payments securely
  • We do NOT store full credit card numbers on our servers
  • Payment tokens are stored by Stripe, not us
  • We only store transaction records (amount, date, status)

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type (for security)
  • Device information (to optimize your experience)
  • Booking patterns and preferences

How We Use Your Information

  • Appointment Management: To schedule, confirm, and manage your bookings
  • Communication: Send reminders, confirmations, and updates about your appointments
  • Payment Processing: Handle deposits and service payments
  • No-Show Protection: Track appointment history to enforce deposit policies
  • Service Improvement: Analyze booking patterns to improve our services
  • Marketing (Optional): Send promotions and updates (only if you opt in)

Data Security

How We Protect Your Data

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Customer data stored in encrypted databases
  • Access Control: Only authorized staff can access customer information
  • Regular Backups: Data backed up securely to prevent loss
  • PCI Compliance: Payment processing meets industry security standards

What We DON'T Do

  • We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data with marketers without consent
  • We do NOT store credit card numbers on our servers
  • We do NOT access your data without legitimate business need

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at (405) 985-0600 or visit our shop.

Data Retention

  • Active Customers: Data retained while you use our services
  • Inactive Accounts: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity (unless legally required)
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for tax/legal purposes
  • No-Show Records: Retained for 1 year to enforce policies

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to:

  • Remember your booking preferences during your session
  • Maintain your login status (if you create an account)
  • Analyze website traffic to improve user experience

We do NOT use cookies for targeted advertising.

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • SendGrid/Mailgun: Email delivery for confirmations
  • Twilio: SMS reminders (if opted in)
  • Google Maps: Location display

These services have their own privacy policies and are bound by strict data protection agreements.
