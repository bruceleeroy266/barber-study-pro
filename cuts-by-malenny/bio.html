The Barber Journey

I've been barbering for 13 years — over a decade of honing my craft, building relationships, and perfecting my technique. The art of transformation, the connection with clients, and the satisfaction of a perfect cut still drive me every day. From classic styles to modern trends, my experience means I can deliver exactly what you're looking for.

Family & Future

Building Cuts by Malenny is a dream I'm pursuing with my fiancé Gabe. Together we're creating something meaningful — a business built on quality, trust, and community. Family is at the heart of everything we do.

Every client who sits in my chair deserves my full attention. No rushed cuts, no cutting corners. Whether you're here for a quick trim or the full KINGS VIP experience, you'll get the same level of care and craftsmanship. I believe a fresh cut isn't just about looking good — it's about walking out ready to take on the world.
