Our Policies

Important information about appointments

💳

Booking Deposits

A $10 deposit is required to secure your appointment.

  • The deposit is applied toward your service total
  • Payable online when booking
  • Cash deposits accepted for walk-ins
📅

Cancellations

We understand plans change. Here's how cancellations work:

  • 24+ hours notice: Full deposit refund
  • 12-24 hours notice: 50% deposit refund
  • Less than 12 hours: Deposit forfeited

To cancel, call or text (405) 985-0600

⏰

Late Arrivals

We respect your time and ask that you respect ours:

  • 15-minute grace period included
  • After 15 minutes, service may be shortened
  • After 20 minutes, appointment may be forfeited
  • Late arrivals may require rescheduling
⚠️

No-Show Policy

A "no-show" is when you miss your appointment without notice.

  • Deposit is forfeited
  • Second no-show: Prepayment required for future bookings
  • Third no-show: Service refusal

We send reminder texts 24 hours before your appointment.

🔄

Rescheduling

Need to change your appointment time?

  • Reschedule with 24+ hours notice at no charge
  • Less than 24 hours: Deposit transfers to new appointment
  • Reschedule via text or call
👶

Children's Services

Kids 12 and under receive $5 off regular haircut pricing.

  • Parent/guardian must be present
  • Please arrive with clean, detangled hair
  • Bring a favorite toy or device for comfort
✨

Satisfaction Guarantee

Your satisfaction is our priority.

  • Free adjustments within 7 days of service
  • Must contact us within 48 hours of concern
  • Applies to cuts performed by Cuts by Malenny
🏥

Health & Safety

We maintain a clean, safe environment for everyone.

  • All tools sanitized between clients
  • Fresh capes and towels for each client
  • Please inform us of any skin conditions or allergies
  • Stay home if you're feeling unwell

Questions?

If you have any questions about our policies, please don't hesitate to reach out.
