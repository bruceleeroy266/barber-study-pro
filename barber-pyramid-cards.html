📋 How to Play

The Goal

Get your partner to guess as many barbering terms as possible before time runs out!

✓ You CAN:

  • Describe the term
  • Use synonyms
  • Act it out
  • Give examples

✗ You CANNOT:

  • Say the word itself
  • Use "rhymes with" or "sounds like"
  • Use parts of the word (e.g., "hair" for "haircut")
  • Use gestures that spell out the word

Scoring

  • Each correct guess: 10 points
  • Complete all 6 words: Bonus 20 points
  • Time limit: 60 seconds per round
