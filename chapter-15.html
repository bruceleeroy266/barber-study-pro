CHAPTER 15

Men's Hair Replacement

Hair systems, consultation, application, and maintenance

🎯 Why Study Hair Replacement?

Hair replacement systems help men with thinning or baldness regain natural-looking hair. Barbers can:

  • Provide honest consultations
  • Measure, fit, cut, style, and maintain systems
  • Increase client loyalty and shop revenue
Approach consultations sensitively; many men feel self-conscious about hair loss.

💇 Types of Hair Used

Human Hair:

  • Most popular for natural look, texture, movement
  • Tolerates chemical services (color, perm)
  • Reacts to climate, fades with sun, requires maintenance

Synthetic Hair:

  • Less costly, holds style well, resistant to humidity
  • Can look shiny or artificial; harder to blend

Mixed Hair:

  • Blend of human and synthetic
  • Balance of natural appearance and durability

📦 Stock vs. Custom Systems

Stock (Ready-Made):

  • Pre-manufactured in standard sizes and colors
  • Faster delivery and lower cost
  • Barber measures, trims, and customizes to fit

Custom Systems:

  • Made to client's exact head shape, color, density
  • More expensive but best, most natural fit
  • Requires template or mold

🔧 Application Methods

Full Head Bonding:

  • Perform patch test 24 hours prior
  • Clean and disinfect scalp
  • Apply multiple thin coats of adhesive
  • Position system carefully and press firmly

Tape Application:

  • Use double-sided tape on reinforced areas
  • Never apply tape directly on lace
  • Use solvent to loosen tape for removal; never pull

📝 Chapter 15 Key Takeaways

  • Human hair provides most natural look; synthetic is more affordable
  • Stock systems are cost-effective; custom systems offer best fit
  • Accurate measuring and templating is essential
  • Always patch test adhesives 24 hours before application
  • Cut and blend conservatively to match natural hair
  • Follow manufacturer guidelines for cleaning, coloring, and perming
