### Why Study Electricity for Barbers (from source):
**Critical Safety Reasons:**
- Barbers use electrical appliances (clippers, trimmers, dryers, etc.)
- Need to know how to safely use electricity and electrical equipment
- Must understand proper equipment operation and maintenance
- Must understand when/how to repair electrical tools safely
- Improper use can cause blown circuit breakers, electric shocks, fires, personal injury
- Need to understand periodic inspections to minimize fire risk from overloaded outlets

**Light-therapy Treatments:**
- Different light rays have different effects on skin
- Barbers need to know appropriate application
- Understanding thermal and chemical properties of light
