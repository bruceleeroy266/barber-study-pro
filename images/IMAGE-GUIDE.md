## How to Download and Use

### Step 1: Search
1. Go to unsplash.com, pexels.com, or pixabay.com
2. Enter search terms from above
3. Select high-quality, relevant images

### Step 2: Download
1. Click download button
2. Choose appropriate size (1200x800px minimum)
3. Save with descriptive filename

### Step 3: Organize
Save images to correct folder:
```
barber-study-pro/images/
├── chapter-06/
│   ├── skeleton-full.jpg
│   ├── skull-anatomy.jpg
│   └── facial-muscles.jpg
```
