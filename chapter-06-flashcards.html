General Anatomy & Physiology

Anatomy

The study of the structure of the human body and its parts. Essential for understanding how barbering services affect the body.

2/18

Physiology

Click to reveal

The study of how the body and its parts function and work together. Understanding functions helps provide better services.

3/18

Cells

Click to reveal

The basic unit of all living things. Cells perform all life processes and make up tissues, organs, and body systems.

4/18

Tissues

Click to reveal

Groups of similar cells that perform a specific function. Four main types: connective, epithelial, muscle, and nerve.

5/18

Organs

Click to reveal

Structures composed of two or more tissue types that work together to perform a specific function in the body.

6/18

Body Systems

Click to reveal

Groups of organs that work together to perform related functions. The body has 11 major systems.

7/18

Skeletal System

Click to reveal

The physical foundation of the body consisting of 206 bones. Provides structure, protects organs, and enables movement.

8/18

Muscular System

Click to reveal

Covers, shapes, and supports the skeleton. Enables body movement and includes over 600 individual muscles.

9/18

Nervous System

Click to reveal

Controls and coordinates all body functions. Includes brain, spinal cord, and nerves that carry messages.

10/18

Circulatory System

Click to reveal

Transports blood, nutrients, and oxygen throughout the body. Includes heart, blood vessels, and blood.

11/18

Lymphatic System

Click to reveal

Helps the body fight infection and disease. Includes lymph nodes, lymph vessels, and lymph fluid.

12/18

Integumentary System

Click to reveal

The skin and its accessory organs including hair, nails, and glands. The body's largest organ system.

13/18

Respiratory System

Click to reveal

Enables breathing and oxygen exchange. Includes lungs, trachea, and air passages.

14/18

Digestive System

Click to reveal

Breaks down food into nutrients the body can use. Includes mouth, stomach, and intestines.

15/18

Endocrine System

Click to reveal

Produces hormones that regulate body functions. Includes glands like thyroid and adrenal glands.

16/18

Excretory System

Click to reveal

Removes waste products from the body. Includes kidneys, bladder, and skin (sweat glands).

17/18

Reproductive System

Click to reveal

Responsible for producing offspring. Includes organs that produce sex cells and hormones.

18/18

Homeostasis

Click to reveal

The body's ability to maintain a stable internal environment despite external changes. Essential for health.
