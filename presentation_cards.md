## 📝 KEY POINTS: CONSULTATION

**What it does:**
- Lets clients ask questions & share expectations
- Time to recommend at-home skincare routines & products
- Learn about contraindications from intake forms

---

## 🔍 KEY POINTS: SKIN ANALYSIS PROCEDURE

1. Review client forms
2. Discuss any questions
3. Client puts on smock
4. Remove jewelry
5. Drape hair
6. Cover client's eyes with cotton pads
7. Use magnifying lamp to observe skin **before & after** cleansing

**Purpose:** Determine skin type, condition, and needed treatment

---

## 🧴 SKIN TYPES TO IDENTIFY

- Oily
- Dry
- Normal
- Combination (dry)
- Combination (oily)
- Acne

## ❓ Q&A CARDS

**Q: What is a client consultation?**  
A: Communication that determines client needs and how to achieve desired results.

---

**Q: Why is the Client Intake Form important?**  
A: Identifies contraindications or health concerns that may require avoiding certain treatments/products.

---

**Q: What is a skin analysis?**  
A: The facial phase where eyes are covered and skin is observed under a mag-lamp before and after cleansing.

---

**Q: What does skin analysis help determine?**  
A: Skin type and conditions to create the best treatment plan and home care recommendations.
