# Expense Tracker Template

## How to Use This
1. Log every business expense here
2. Categorize correctly (affects taxes)
3. Save receipts — I can help organize
4. Monthly: I'll summarize for you

## Categories (Tax-Deductible for Business)
- **Advertising** — Business cards, flyers, social media ads
- **Insurance** — Liability, property, workers comp
- **Interest** — Business loan interest
- **Legal/Professional** — Lawyer, accountant, consultant fees
- **Office Expense** — Supplies, software, small equipment
- **Rent/Lease** — Space, equipment leases
- **Repairs/Maintenance** — Equipment fixes, space upkeep
- **Supplies** — Products you sell or use (coffee beans, barber products)
- **Taxes/Licenses** — Business licenses, permits
- **Travel** — Business-related trips
- **Meals** — 50% deductible if business purpose
- **Utilities** — Electric, gas, water, internet, phone
- **Wages** — Employee pay + payroll taxes
- **Contract Labor** — 1099 workers
