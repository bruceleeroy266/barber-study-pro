Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! During our launch period, all 21 chapters and 1,050+ quiz questions are completely free. We're building our user base and collecting feedback before switching to premium pricing.

What happens when you go premium?

Users who sign up during the free period will keep their access. New users will need to purchase a subscription or one-time license. We haven't finalized pricing yet, but it will be affordable (think $29-39, not $75 like Milady).

Is this only for Oklahoma?

Currently, yes. The content is tailored to the NIC (National-Interstate Council) exam used in Oklahoma. However, many states use the NIC exam, so the content may be helpful elsewhere. We're expanding to other states soon!

Can I study offline?

Yes! You can download the complete study guide as a ZIP file and study without internet access. Perfect for studying on the go.

How do I know this will help me pass?

This study guide covers all the same material as your textbook (Milady Standard Barbering), just in a more accessible, mobile-friendly format. Plus, with 1,050+ practice questions, you'll be well-prepared for the exam format.
