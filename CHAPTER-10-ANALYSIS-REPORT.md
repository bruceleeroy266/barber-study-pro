### Key Concepts from Source:

**Trichology Definition:**
- Scientific study of hair, disorders, and care
- From Greek: trichos ("hair") + ology ("study of")
- Hair is appendage of integumentary system
- Protects head from heat/cold
- Source of adornment and personal style expression

**Why Study Hair & Scalp (Critical for Barbers):**
1. Know how products and services affect hair structure
2. Keep client's hair and scalp healthy
3. Differentiate normal vs. abnormal conditions
4. Perform scalp and hair analysis before services

**Hair Structure Basics:**
- **Hair root:** Portion enclosed within follicle beneath skin surface
- **Hair shaft:** Visible portion above skin surface
- Hair grows from root, not from shaft end

**Figure 10-1 Components:**
- Hair follicle
- Hair root
- Dermal papilla
- Arrector pili muscle
- Sebaceous glands
