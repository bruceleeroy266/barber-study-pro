Professionalism

The conduct, aims, or qualities that characterize a professional person. Includes competence, reliability, and ethical behavior.

2/18

Personal Grooming

Click to reveal

The practice of maintaining cleanliness and appearance of one's body. Essential for barbers who serve as role models.

3/18

Uniform

Click to reveal

Standard work attire that identifies you as a professional barber. Should be clean, pressed, and appropriate for the workplace.

4/18

Posture

Click to reveal

The position in which someone holds their body. Good posture projects confidence and prevents physical strain during work.

5/18

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Click to reveal

Equipment worn to minimize exposure to hazards. In barbering includes gloves, aprons, and eye protection.

6/18

Client Consultation

Click to reveal

Discussion between barber and client to determine desired services, assess hair condition, and establish expectations.

7/18

Client Record Card

Click to reveal

Document containing client information including contact details, service history, product preferences, and allergies.

8/18

Telephone Etiquette

Click to reveal

Proper manners when answering calls including greeting, identifying the business, speaking clearly, and being helpful.

9/18

Appointment Book

Click to reveal

System for scheduling client services. Can be physical book or digital software. Essential for time management.

10/18

Retail Sales

Click to reveal

Selling hair care products to clients. Important revenue stream and helps clients maintain their style between visits.

11/18

Product Knowledge

Click to reveal

Understanding the features, benefits, and usage of hair care products. Necessary for making appropriate recommendations.

12/18

Rebooking

Click to reveal

Scheduling a client's next appointment before they leave. Helps maintain steady clientele and predictable income.

13/18

Client Retention

Click to reveal

The ability to keep clients returning for services. Built through excellent service, consistency, and relationship building.

14/18

Professional Boundaries

Click to reveal

Limits that protect the professional relationship between barber and client. Maintain appropriate personal distance.

15/18

Confidentiality

Click to reveal

The ethical duty to keep client information private. Never discuss clients or their personal matters with others.

16/18

Conflict Resolution

Click to reveal

The process of resolving disagreements or complaints. Listen actively, apologize sincerely, and find solutions.

17/18

Upselling

Click to reveal

Suggesting additional services or products that benefit the client. Should be done ethically and appropriately.

18/18

Service Menu

Click to reveal

List of services offered with descriptions and prices. Should be clearly displayed and kept current.
