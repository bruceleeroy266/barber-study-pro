## Key Findings from Reddit/Forums

### What Students Complain About:
1. **"Schools do nothing to help prepare"** - Common complaint
2. **High fail rates** - CA pass rate ~50%, apprentices ~30%
3. **Need unlimited mock tests** - Students want to practice repeatedly
4. **Want to track missed questions** - Focus on weak areas
5. **State-specific content** - Generic prep isn't enough

### What Students Want:
- Mock tests "almost identical to state test"
- Track what they get wrong
- Study weak areas specifically
- Start studying early (not cram)
- Affordable options

## Feature Gaps (What Competitors DON'T Have)

### 1. School Admin Dashboard ❌
- NONE of the competitors have this
- Schools can't track student progress
- No at-risk student identification
- No bulk student management

### 2. State-Specific Question Banks ❌
- All use generic NIC questions
- States have different laws/exam weights
- No customization per state

### 3. Practical Exam Video Content ❌
- All focus on written exam only
- No technique demonstrations
- No practical skill videos

### 4. Real Student Progress Tracking ❌
- No chapter-by-chapter progress
- No time spent metrics
- No engagement analytics

### 5. Affordable B2B Pricing ❌
- Pivot Point = expensive ($21K+)
- Others = B2C only, no school plans
- Gap for $500-2000/year school licenses
