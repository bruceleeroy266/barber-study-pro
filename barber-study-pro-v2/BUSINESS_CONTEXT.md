# Barber Study Pro - Business Context

## Product Vision
**Barber Study Pro** is a B2B SaaS study platform designed for barber schools to help their students pass state board exams.

### Target Market
- Barber schools (B2B, not B2C)
- Cosmetology schools with barber programs
- State board exam preparation

### Revenue Model
- Subscription or license-based
- Per-student or per-school pricing
- White-label/custom branding options

### Key Differentiators Needed
1. School admin dashboard with student progress tracking
2. State-specific content (not generic)
3. Practical exam video content
4. Better UX than competitors
5. Integration with school LMS systems

### Competitive Landscape
See competitive analysis in project documentation.

### Status
- MVP in development
- 21 chapters with content and quizzes complete
- Need: Admin dashboard, practice exams, state-specific content

### Next Steps
1. Build admin dashboard for schools
2. Create full practice exam engine
3. Add state-specific question banks
4. Develop practical exam video content
5. Beta test with local barber schools
