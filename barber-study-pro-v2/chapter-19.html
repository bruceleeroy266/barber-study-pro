CHAPTER 19

Preparing for Licensure & Employment

State board exam, resume, interview, and job search

🎯 Why Study This Chapter?

  • You must pass state board exam (written + practical) to become licensed
  • A successful job search is a job in itself
  • Proper preparation gives you the edge

📝 Preparing for Licensure

Written Exam:

  • Develop good study habits and time management
  • Create realistic study schedule
  • Keep organized notes and vocabulary list
  • Review past quizzes/tests
  • Know your state's barber laws

Test-Taking Strategies:

  • Read all directions carefully
  • Budget your time; wear a watch
  • Answer easy questions first; mark skipped ones
  • Eliminate known incorrect answers
  • Watch for qualifying words (always, never, usually)

Practical Exam:

  • Practice required skills repeatedly
  • Participate in mock exams
  • Prepare checklist of required equipment
  • Follow all infection control procedures
  • Present neat, clean, professional appearance

💼 Employment Preparation

Resume Development:

  • Keep to one page
  • Use clean, professional formatting
  • Include contact info, education, experience, skills
  • Use action verbs (achieved, developed, increased)
  • Attach cover letter

Employment Portfolio:

  • Diplomas and certificates
  • Awards and achievements
  • Resume and references
  • Before-and-after photos
  • Continuing education certificates

Interview Tips:

  • Arrive early
  • Turn off cell phone
  • Project confidence and professionalism
  • Answer honestly
  • Never criticize former employers
  • Ask prepared questions
  • Send thank-you note after

📝 Chapter 19 Key Takeaways

  • Licensure: Master content + develop test-wise strategies for written and practical exams
  • Know your state's barber laws
  • Employment: Prepare strong resume, portfolio, and cover letter
  • Interview: Dress professionally, arrive early, answer honestly, ask questions, follow up
  • Success comes from preparation, persistence, and positive attitude
