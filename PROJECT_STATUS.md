# Barber Study Pro - Project Status

## Project Overview
**Product:** Barber Study Pro - B2B SaaS study platform for barber schools
**Goal:** Help barber school students pass state board exams
**Target Market:** Barber schools (B2B), individual students (B2C)
**Revenue Model:** Subscription/license-based

## Current Status: Phase 2 COMPLETE ✅

### Files Created:
1. **index.html** - Main landing page with 21 chapter links
2. **chapter-01.html through chapter-21.html** - Complete study content with mini quizzes
3. **practice-exam.html** - 50-question simulated exam with timer
4. **admin-dashboard.html** - School admin portal (login, stats, student tracking)
5. **missed-questions.html** - Review bank for incorrect answers
6. **my-progress.html** - Student progress tracking with achievements
7. **COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md** - Competitor pricing and feature gaps
8. **BUSINESS_CONTEXT.md** - Business model and strategy

### Features Implemented:
- ✅ 21 chapters with comprehensive content
- ✅ Mini quizzes per chapter (3 questions each)
- ✅ Full practice exam (50 questions, 90-min timer)
- ✅ School admin dashboard with student tracking
- ✅ Missed questions bank with mastery tracking
- ✅ Student progress page with achievements
- ✅ LocalStorage persistence for progress
- ✅ Responsive design (mobile-friendly)
- ✅ Dark theme with gold accents

### Pricing Strategy:
- **Free:** 1 chapter + sample exam
- **Student:** $19.99 (undercuts competitors at $27-60)
- **School:** $999/year unlimited students
- **Enterprise:** $2,999/year white-label
