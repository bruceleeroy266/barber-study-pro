CHAPTER 21

The Business of Barbering

Ownership options, business plan, operations, and marketing

🎯 Why Study Business?

  • You may become a booth renter or shop owner
  • Understanding business helps manage your career
  • Essential for attracting and retaining employees and clients

🏢 Types of Business Ownership

Sole Proprietorship:

  • Owner makes all decisions
  • Assumes all expenses, receives all profits, bears all losses
  • Best if you want full control

Partnership:

  • Two or more people share ownership
  • More capital, shared skills/responsibilities
  • Partners share liability for debts

Corporation:

  • Controlled by stockholders
  • Limits personal liability
  • Greater flexibility, easier to raise capital

Franchise:

  • Contract with successful franchisor
  • Brand recognition and marketing support
  • Fees and strict stipulations; less independence

📋 Business Plan Essentials

  • Executive summary
  • Vision and mission statements
  • Organizational plan
  • Marketing plan
  • Financial documents (projected and actual)
  • Supporting documents and policies
Create a Vision Statement (long-term picture) and Mission Statement (guides actions) with specific goals.

💰 Money Allocation (Benchmarks)

  • Salaries/Commissions (including payroll taxes): ~53.5%
  • Rent: ~13%
  • Supplies: ~5%
  • Advertising: ~3%
  • Other expenses: ~7.5%
  • Total Expenses: ~85%
  • Net Profit: ~15%

📢 Building Your Business

  • Social Media — Consistent username, get permission for photos, post regularly
  • Advertising — Budget ≤3% of gross income
  • Referrals — Satisfied clients are best advertising
  • Retailing — Focus on client needs/benefits
  • Prebooking — Schedule next appointment before client leaves
Always prioritize client satisfaction — happy clients are your best marketing.

📝 Chapter 21 Key Takeaways

  • Success requires balancing artistic skill with business knowledge
  • Evaluate ownership options (sole prop, partnership, corporation, franchise)
  • A solid business plan, brand identity, location, legal compliance are essential
  • Efficient operations, excellent layout, strong personnel management drive profitability
  • Build business through social media, advertising, referrals, professional selling
  • Always prioritize client satisfaction
