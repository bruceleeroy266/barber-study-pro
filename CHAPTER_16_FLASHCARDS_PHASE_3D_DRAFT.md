## 59. Subsection Size in Graduated Cutting

**Front:**  
Why should subsections remain narrow and controlled in a graduated cut?

**Back:**

## 62. When to Avoid Overdirection

**Front:**  
When should **overdirection** be avoided?

**Back:**  
Overdirection should be avoided when the goal is balanced, natural fall or even weight distribution, because it intentionally shifts length and weight.

## 67. Airflow Direction in Blow-Drying

**Front:**  
How does **airflow direction** affect blow-dry finish?

**Back:**  
Airflow directed upward at the roots creates volume. Airflow directed downward along the cuticle creates smoothness and shine.
